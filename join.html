How to Join N2XT

Follow these steps to become part of our elite team

01

Submit Application

Fill out our detailed player application form with your gaming history, achievements, and preferred roles.

  • Include competitive experience
  • Add tournament results
  • Provide gaming profiles
  • Upload highlight clips
02

Skill Assessment

Our coaching staff reviews applications and invites top candidates for skill assessment tryouts.

  • Aim and mechanical skill tests
  • Game sense evaluation
  • Communication assessment
  • Performance under pressure
03

Team Tryouts

Successful candidates join team tryouts to evaluate synergy and teamwork with current N2XT players.

  • Scrimmage sessions
  • Strategy development
  • Team communication
  • Competitive mindset
04

Final Decision

Management makes final decisions based on performance, team fit, and long-term potential.

  • Contract negotiations
  • Training schedule setup
  • Team introduction
  • Media preparation

Player Requirements

Skill Level

  • Top 500 in your region
  • Minimum 1.2 K/D ratio
  • Tournament experience
  • Consistent performance

Availability

  • 30+ hours weekly training
  • Weekend tournament availability
  • Streaming commitments
  • Team practice sessions

Teamwork

  • Positive attitude
  • Strong communication
  • Adaptability
  • Coachable mindset
